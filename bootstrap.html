Dr. Emily Carter

Her passion for physical therapy began after sustaining a severe knee injury in college, which introduced her to the challenges of recovery firsthand. Determined to bridge the gap between therapy and fitness, she pursued a Doctorate in Physical Therapy (DPT) and worked in leading sports rehabilitation clinics before co-founding HealFit. Dr. Carter is the driving force behind the company’s evidence-based approach, ensuring all programs are grounded in the latest research and clinical expertise.

Ryan Bennett

Ryan Bennett is a former tech entrepreneur with a passion for innovation and fitness. After selling his first start-up in the wearable tech space, he wanted to focus on building technology that could positively impact people's lives. Ryan was inspired to create HealFit after watching his mother struggle with her recovery process following a hip replacement. Combining his expertise in technology with his drive to make recovery tools more accessible, Ryan designed HealFit’s digital platform, which seamlessly integrates virtual therapy, progress tracking, and personalized fitness plans.

Sophia Martinez

Sophia Martinez is a certified fitness trainer and wellness advocate who specializes in working with individuals recovering from injuries and surgeries. After years of running her own fitness studio, Sophia saw a gap in the market for recovery-focused fitness solutions. She co-founded HealFit to create a supportive, inclusive environment for people at every stage of healing. Sophia’s mission is to empower clients by focusing on sustainable fitness routines that prioritize mental and physical well-being. Her passion for community-building led to HealFit’s virtual support groups and wellness workshops.
